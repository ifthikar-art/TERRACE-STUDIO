Welcome

Your Home Away From Home
in Mandapam

Cozy terrace studio ideal for 2–6 guests family. Located 20 km before the Rameswaram Temple and just 4 km from the iconic Pamban Bridge, it's a convenient and peaceful stay.

Both rooms are comfortable, air-conditioned, and situated on the terrace. With a kitchen and dining area, we live on the ground floor and are available if needed. Home-cooked food is provided on request, and covered parking is available. Simple, clean, and welcoming.

Guests have access to the private terrace rooms, as well as the terrace and garden areas. These spaces are perfect for relaxing, enjoying fresh air, or spending quiet time outdoors during your stay.
